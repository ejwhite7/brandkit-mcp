# Brand Voice & Tone

Describe your brand personality and voice guidelines here.

## Voice Attributes

| We Sound Like | We Don't Sound Like |
|---|---|
| Confident | Arrogant |
| Clear | Jargon-heavy |
| Friendly | Overly casual |

## Writing Principles

1. Use active voice
2. Write in plain language
3. Be specific
4. Lead with the benefit
