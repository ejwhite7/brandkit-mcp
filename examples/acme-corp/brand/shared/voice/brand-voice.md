# Acme Corp Brand Voice & Tone

## Brand Personality

Acme Corp is a trusted leader in enterprise solutions. Our brand embodies:

- **Innovative**: We push boundaries and embrace new ideas
- **Reliable**: We deliver on promises consistently
- **Approachable**: We make complex things simple
- **Confident**: We speak with authority, not arrogance

## Voice Attributes

| We Sound Like | We Don't Sound Like |
|---|---|
| Confident and capable | Arrogant or dismissive |
| Clear and direct | Jargon-heavy or vague |
| Warm and human | Cold or robotic |
| Forward-looking | Stuck in the past |
| Expert but accessible | Condescending or patronizing |

## Tone Variations

- **Marketing pages**: Inspiring, forward-looking, benefit-oriented
- **Product UI**: Clear, concise, action-oriented
- **Documentation**: Thorough, patient, educational
- **Error messages**: Empathetic, solution-focused, specific
- **Social media**: Conversational, energetic, shareable

## Writing Principles

1. **Lead with the benefit** -- Tell people what they gain, not what we do
2. **Use active voice** -- "BrandKit indexes your files" not "Files are indexed"
3. **Be specific** -- "Indexes in under 1 second" not "Really fast"
4. **Keep it scannable** -- Short paragraphs, clear headings, bullet points
5. **Show, don't tell** -- Use examples and demos over descriptions
