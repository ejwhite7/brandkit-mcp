# Acme Corp Logo Usage

## Logo Variants

- **Primary**: Full color logo on light backgrounds. Use for most applications.
- **Reversed**: White logo for dark backgrounds and overlays.
- **Mark**: The "A" icon symbol only. Use when space is limited or as a favicon.
- **Wordmark**: "Acme Corp" text only. Use in text-heavy contexts.
- **Monochrome**: Single-color version for special print applications.

## Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "A" in the logo mark on all sides.
This ensures the logo remains prominent and uncluttered.

## Minimum Sizes

- **Full logo**: 120px wide (digital), 1 inch (print)
- **Logo mark**: 24px (digital), 0.25 inch (print)

## Forbidden Usage

- Do not stretch, rotate, or skew the logo
- Do not add drop shadows, gradients, or effects
- Do not place on visually busy backgrounds
- Do not change the logo colors outside of approved variants
- Do not recreate the logo with different fonts
- Do not crop or partially obscure the logo
