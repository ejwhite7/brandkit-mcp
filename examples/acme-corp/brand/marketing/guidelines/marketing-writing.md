# Marketing Content Guidelines

## Headlines

- Keep under 10 words
- Lead with the benefit
- Use power verbs: Build, Create, Launch, Scale, Transform

## CTAs

- Primary CTA: action-oriented ("Get Started", "Try Free", "See Demo")
- Secondary CTA: exploratory ("Learn More", "See How It Works")
- Never use generic CTAs like "Click Here" or "Submit"

## Page Structure

1. Hero with value proposition
2. Social proof (logos, testimonials, stats)
3. Feature showcase (3-4 key features)
4. Detailed benefits section
5. Customer stories or case studies
6. Pricing or trial CTA
7. Final CTA section
