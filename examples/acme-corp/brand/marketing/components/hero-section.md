# Hero Section

Full-width hero section for landing pages and the homepage.

## Usage

Use on the top of landing pages and the homepage. Always includes a primary heading, supporting text, and at least one CTA button.

## Variants

### Standard
Full-width with gradient background, centered text, and CTA buttons.

### Video Background
Same as standard but with a looping background video.

### Split Layout
Left side has text content, right side has an image or illustration.

## Specifications

- Height: minimum 60vh, maximum 80vh
- Background: gradient from --color-hero-gradient-start to --color-hero-gradient-end
- Heading: --font-family-heading at --font-size-5xl or --font-size-6xl
- Body: --font-family-primary at --font-size-lg
- CTA: Primary button style, minimum 48px height
- Padding: 4rem horizontal, centered with max-width 1200px
